About Project

Scroller is a simple social media app that users can post video instantly. The app is built in MVC architecture with Get X as state management and firebasae as backend. Users can sign up with their email id. Also this app supports google sign in and signup. After registering in the app users can search for other users and you can like every post and comment and share and more.
